# GoogleBusinessProfile Rules

This is a planned WebPresence Capability. Until `Prompt.md` exists and the
Capability is promoted, use it as boundary guidance only.

## Data Boundaries

- May read owner-approved business facts in repository Markdown.
- May cite official Google help links.
- May write repository Markdown checklists, notes, and plans.
- Must not store Google Account passwords, verification codes, recovery details,
  payment details, or private profile credentials.
- Must not enter or change external Google Business Profile data without a
  separate owner-approved external-profile pass.

## Claims Boundaries

- Do not promise rankings, leads, calls, map placement, revenue, or verification.
- Do not imply Google endorses the repository, owner, or service.
- Do not invent business name, address, service area, category, hours, phone
  number, website URL, photos, or ownership facts.
- Do not advise incentives for reviews.
- Use official links as references, not guarantees.

## Planned Promotion

Before promotion to Active:

1. Create `Prompt.md` from approved source content.
2. Add a setup/readiness checklist that writes only to repository Markdown.
3. Confirm official links are current.
4. Update registry, router, and guide rows.
5. Record changelog and related WebPresence pack row.
