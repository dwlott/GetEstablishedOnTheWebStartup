# Starter Product Goals

This repository is **GetEstablishedOnTheWebStartup** — a web-presence starter
for your goals, plans, example content, and Capability workflows.

## Goals

1. **Support Quick Startup** — owner goals, preferences, and one clear next task.
2. **Provide example website Markdown** — adaptable drafts under `Content/Website/Pages/`.
3. **Enable one-page business website drafting** — **OnePageWebsite** Capability.
4. **Plan local profiles safely** — Google and Yelp Capabilities remain **Planned**
   until prompts and claims rules are complete.
5. **Stay Git-ready** — connect your own GitHub repo via **GitHubWorkflow** when ready.
6. **Grow on demand** — use **RepositoryScaffold** for folders; avoid pre-shipping
   production build tooling.

## Not Goals For This Starter

- Operating GetEstablishedOnTheWeb.com product publishing
- Local WordPress build automation or production launch execution
- Storing owner credentials or private mirror paths from the product repo
