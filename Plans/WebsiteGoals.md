# Website Goals

Canonical home: **GetEstablishedOnTheWeb** product repository (`Content/Website/`).

These are **website outcomes** — separate from the GetEstablished **learning
repository** goals at `C:\Repositories\GetEstablished\Plans\RepositoryGoals.md`.

## North Star

GetEstablishedOnTheWeb.com helps people, businesses, bands, and projects **use
AI to get established on the web the easy way**. The site leads with clear,
straightforward workflows for practical basics — Google, Yelp, Apple Maps, and
the other places customers look — then introduces **GetEstablishedOnTheWebStartup**
as the AI-ready repository built on GetEstablished, with web Capabilities that
make planning, publishing, and growing easier over time.

## Goals

### 1. Visitor-first practical content

Lead with what people search for: getting on Google, setting up Yelp, and other
sensible web-presence basics. Platform and repository depth follow — not the
reverse.

### 2. Publish-ready content

All core public pages meet
[PublicWebsitePageStructurePlan.md](PublicWebsitePageStructurePlan.md). Google
and Yelp basics pages are lead content, not buried deferrals.

### 3. Clear calls to action

Dual path:

- **Practical path:** Start Here → Google / Yelp basics pages.
- **Repository path:** **GetEstablishedStartup** (free magnet) and
  **GetEstablishedOnTheWebStartup** (paid web product) as **parallel** offers;
  specialty repos join the same shelf as they ship.

No overpromising on pricing, contact forms, or unreleased companion repos.

### 4. Primary site live

WordPress on Bluehost with StudioPress Genesis and Altitude theme serves
`getestablishedontheweb.com` from approved Markdown source. Local WAMP staging
complete — see [LocalWordPressBuildReport.md](LocalWordPressBuildReport.md).

### 5. Track separation

Public GEOTW site copy stays distinct from user **one-page business website**
outputs (`Content/OnePageBusinessWebsite/` when used).

### 6. Repository and ecosystem story

Explain **parallel repository offers**: free **GetEstablishedStartup** magnet,
paid **GetEstablishedOnTheWebStartup**, and future **specialty for-sale repos**
(MoverCalcs, MetaToolExcel, GithubAgenticAICertPrep) — gracefully, without
invented ship dates or pricing.

### 7. Grounded proof

Credibility follows [OpenQuestions.md](OpenQuestions.md): repository-as-proof
first; no unsupported testimonials or AI evaluation hype.

### 8. Low maintenance

Markdown in this repo remains canonical for page copy; WordPress edits require a
documented sync rule to avoid drift.

### 9. Method and Capability catalog (Option A)

Publish the GetEstablished method story and a visitor-facing Capability reference
on this site (`/method/`, `/capabilities/`) — GEOTW remains the lead public actor
for practical web help, repository offers, companion stories, and method depth.
See [MethodWebCatalogPlan.md](MethodWebCatalogPlan.md).
