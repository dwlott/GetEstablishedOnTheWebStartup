# Website Goals

These are **your website outcomes** — separate from the GetEstablishedOnTheWeb
product site and separate from the GetEstablished method host.

## North Star

Build a clear, honest web presence for your business or project — starting with
practical basics (Google, Yelp, sensible first steps) and growing through
reviewable Markdown drafts and Capabilities.

## Goals

### 1. Owner-first practical content

Adapt example pages under `Content/Website/Pages/` for your business. Do not
publish claims you have not verified.

### 2. One-page business website option

Use **OnePageWebsite** when a simple single-page site fits your needs before a
larger site build.

### 3. Local profile planning

Use **GoogleBusinessProfile** and **YelpBusinessProfile** (Planned) for
setup/readiness guidance — no credential storage or ranking promises.

### 4. Low maintenance source

Keep Markdown in this repository as your working source until you approve a
publishing platform pass.

### 5. Grounded proof

Credibility follows [OpenQuestions.md](OpenQuestions.md). Do not invent
testimonials, licensing, or performance guarantees.

- Public launch: **not started** until owner approves
