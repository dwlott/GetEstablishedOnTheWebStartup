# Get Listed

**Get listed where customers look** — one of the first sensible steps to getting established on the web.

You do not need a full website on day one. You need to show up on the directories and maps people already use when they search for a business, a service, or a place to go.

Every **practical listing guide** on this site is a checklist with official links — pick your platform below and work through it one step at a time.

## Where To Get Listed

Start with the platforms your customers are most likely to use. Each page below is self-contained: a basic starting path, a gather-first checklist, and official links only.

### [Get on Google](GoogleBusinessProfileBasics.md)

Claim or create your **Google Business Profile** so your business can appear on Google Search and Maps.

### [Get on Bing](BingPlacesBasics.md)

Claim or create your **Bing Places for Business** listing so your business can appear on Bing Search and Maps.

### [Get on Facebook](FacebookBusinessPageBasics.md)

Create or set up a **Facebook business Page** so customers can find, follow, and message your business.

### [Get on Nextdoor](NextdoorBusinessPageBasics.md)

Claim or create your **Nextdoor Business Page** if your customers are local neighbors, homeowners, or nearby service buyers.

### [Get on Foursquare](FoursquareBusinessListingBasics.md)

Claim or create your **Foursquare business listing** so your location details are accurate in Foursquare's place data.

### [Get on BBB](BetterBusinessBureauProfileBasics.md)

Claim or request a free **BBB Business Profile**, with accreditation as a separate paid decision later.

### [Get on Yelp](YelpBasics.md)

Claim or add your business on **Yelp** — including the one review-policy caution that trips many businesses up.

### [Get on Apple Maps](AppleMapsBasics.md)

Claim or add your location through **Apple Business** so customers find you on Apple Maps.

### [Get on Yellow Pages](YellowPagesBasics.md)

Claim or add your business on **YP.com** (The Real Yellow Pages).

## How These Guides Work

Each listing page gives you:

- A **Basic Starting Path** — numbered steps in sensible order.
- **Official links only** — no third-party shortcuts or unsupported promises.
- A **Basic Information To Gather** checklist so you are ready before you start.

These pages are not a marketing plan and do not promise rankings or leads. They help you move forward without overthinking tools.

## Before You Start

You do not need a formal business plan, but a short sketch helps: what you do, who you serve, and how customers reach you. Gather business name, category, address or service area, phone number, and hours before opening the first official link.

## After You Are Listed

Once you are claimed on Google, Bing, Facebook, Nextdoor, Foursquare, BBB, Yelp, or another directory:

1. Keep hours, phone, website, and photos current.
2. Respond to reviews from the official business account.
3. When you want organized next steps — business planning, page drafts, and AI-assisted workflows — visit **[Use AI](UseAI.md)**.

The listing guides get you visible. **GetEstablished** and this site together help you go faster when you want a structured workspace agents can work from.
