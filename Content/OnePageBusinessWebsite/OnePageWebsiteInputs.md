# One-Page Website Inputs

Use these fields to collect the practical business information needed for a simple one-page website draft.

Keep answers short at first. Unknowns can stay blank or move to `Plans/OpenQuestions.md` if they need human review.

Fields marked **Essential** are the quick-path minimum for a first draft. Fields marked **Optional** can wait and should not block a first one-page draft.

Do not invent business facts, claims, proof, testimonials, credentials, pricing, guarantees, legal language, policy language, contact details, or service areas.

## First Draft Minimum

A first one-page website draft can usually start with:

- Business Name Or Site Title.
- Tagline.
- Short Business Description.
- Services.
- Service Area.
- Why Choose Us.
- About.
- Contact / Preferred Contact Method.
- Call To Action.

Everything else can remain optional until the business owner has enough confirmed information. Missing optional details can stay blank or move to `Plans/OpenQuestions.md`.
