# Valuable References

Capture URLs, documents, or prior repositories the owner wants agents to know
about — without duplicating full content here.

Agents may add **candidate** rows (`Confidence: Suggested`) with a short note on
what each link is for. The owner reviews and promotes rows to
`Confidence: Owner-confirmed`.
